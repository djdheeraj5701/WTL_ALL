ABOUT

We provide the BEST food in the region. Started by a foodie, this restaurant serves mouth-watering dishes.

A food enthusiast had a vision to provide food that tingles taste-buds ( in the yummiest way possible). We are essentially providers of Indian continental food - From sandwiches to burgers to milkshakes to pizzas, we got it all. We live by our tradition and serve food, in the most hygienic and delicious way possible.We take pride in making sure that our hygiene standards are at par with industry regulations.

All our chefs, helpers and vendors from whom we source our materials have gone through careful screening to maintain good hygiene and all our food handlers are medically certified.

The ambience of our restaurant is inspired by urban life with hints of yellow lights and comfy seating for the young fam. Good music-good food and a place to hangout with close friends has made crazy cheesy a youngsters favourite space to chill.In our quest to provide good quality pocket friendly food, we believe sky is the limit, and so we have geared ourselves up to expand our horizons to different localities within city and beyond.
